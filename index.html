I obtained my Licentiate degree in Physics in 2008 and my PhD in Physics in 2013, from the National University of Rosario (UNR, Argentina). From 2013 to 2015 I was a post-doc at the La Plata Astrophysics Institute (IALP), under a CONICET scholarship. In 2015 I was accepted as a CONICET researcher at IALP, were I work to this day. My main field of study are star clusters and I'm particularly interested in the development of astrophysical software using Python.
